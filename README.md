## **1. Entrepreneurship**

Entrepreneurship refers to the **process of starting and managing a business venture**, taking financial risks to achieve profit and growth. Entrepreneurs aim to create value and contribute to society.

**Key Aspects**

* Innovation
* Risk-taking
* Leadership
* Market Understanding
* Persistence & Flexibility
* Resource and Financial Management

---

## **2. Entrepreneur**

An entrepreneur is a person who **identifies opportunities**, takes risks, organizes resources, and leads business creation.

**Peter Drucker:**
*‚ÄúAn entrepreneur searches for change, responds to it, and exploits it as an opportunity.‚Äù*

## **5. Creativity & Entrepreneurship**

* **Creativity** means generating new ideas.
* **Innovation** means turning those ideas into useful products/services.

Creativity helps entrepreneurs **find opportunities where others see problems**.

**Ways to enhance creativity:**

* Brainstorming
* Growth mindset
* Mind-mapping
* Experimentation
* Surrounding with creative people
* Engaging in right-brain activities (music, art, reading)

---

## **6. Idea Generation Techniques**

| Technique         | Explanation                                              |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Brainstorming     | Free flow of ideas without judgment                      |
| Mind Mapping      | Visualizing interconnected ideas                         |
| SCAMPER           | Modify existing ideas (Substitute, Combine, Adapt, etc.) |
| SWOT Analysis     | Evaluate Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats   |
| Customer Feedback | Understanding customer needs directly                    |

---

## **7. Environmental Analysis**

Identifying **external factors** that influence business decisions.

**PEST Analysis**

* Political
* Economic
* Social
* Technological

**SWOT Analysis**

* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Opportunities
* Threats

Also includes:

* Market Trends
* Competitor Analysis
* Regulatory Analysis
* Stakeholder Analysis

---

## **8. Entrepreneurship Ecosystem**

A supportive environment that enables startup creation and growth.

**Components**

* Entrepreneurs
* Incubators & Accelerators
* Co-working Spaces
* Banks, Angel Investors, Venture Capitalists
* Government Policies & Startup Missions
* Universities & Research Institutions
* Corporate Partnerships
* Infrastructure and Technology
* Culture encouraging innovation & risk-taking
